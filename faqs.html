Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about CashIt online banking.

How do I open a CashIt account?

You can open an account online by clicking on the Register button and filling in your details. Verification is quick and secure.

How do I reset my password?

Click on Login, then choose "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.

Is CashIt online banking secure?

Yes. We use advanced encryption, two-factor authentication, and continuous monitoring to ensure your data and money are safe.

Can I transfer money to other banks?

Absolutely. You can transfer funds to other banks in Pakistan using IBFT or RTGS through our online banking portal or mobile app.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via phone at (555) 123-4567, email at help@cashit.com, or use the live chat available on our website.

Are there any fees for using online banking?

Most online banking services are free. Some transactions like international transfers may incur a small fee. Check our fees & charges page for details.
